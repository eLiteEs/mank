SHOW
===============
Displays the details of a specific commit: its author, date, message,
and a diff of what changed compared to its parent commit.

Usage:
    mank show <hash>

Examples:
    mank show a1b2c3d4e5f6...     Show a commit by its full hash.
    mank show a1b2c3d4            Short hashes also work if unambiguous.

Output:
    - Commit hash, author, date and parent.
    - Commit message.
    - Per-file diff: lines added in green, removed in red.
    - Files deleted in this commit are listed at the end.

Notes:
    - Output is shown through a pager. Use arrow keys or Page Up/Down
      to scroll, and press q to exit.
    - You can get commit hashes from "mank log" or "mank log --oneline".
