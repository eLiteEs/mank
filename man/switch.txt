SWITCH
===============
Switches the working tree to another branch.

Usage:
    mank switch <branch>
    mank -sw <branch>

Examples:
    mank switch main
    mank switch feature-login

Notes:
    - The branch must exist before switching to it. Create it first
      with "mank branch <name>".
    - Uncommitted changes may conflict when switching. Stash them
      first with "mank stash" if needed.
