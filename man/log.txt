LOG
===============
Displays the commit history of the current branch, from the most
recent commit to the oldest.

Usage:
    mank log
    mank log --oneline
    mank -l
    mank -l --oneline

Modes:
    (default)       Shows full details for each commit: hash, date,
                    author, email and message, separated by a divider.

    --oneline       Shows a compact view with the short hash and
                    message on a single line per commit.

Examples:
    mank log
    mank log --oneline
